Tribute to A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam, also known as the Missile Man of India, was an inspirational scientist and leader who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.

Born on 15th October 1931, in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, he played a key role in India's civilian space program and military missile development efforts, earning him the title of the Missile Man. He was also a beloved teacher, who spent his later years inspiring students across the country.

Dr. Kalam was a man of simplicity and humility, and he dedicated his life to the service of the nation and its people. His contributions to science, education, and public service continue to inspire generations.
